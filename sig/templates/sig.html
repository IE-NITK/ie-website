Code

Gadget

IE Gadget is an assembly of talented individuals from diverse technical backgrounds who share a common love for circuitry and computation. We are a group of ‘heavily doped’ and motivated individuals who learn and propagate practical electronics. In other words, the more the LEDs we burn, the more pleasure we derive.

Garage

IE Garage is the oldest special interest group of the IE NITK chapter. We are a group of combobulated techies from core engineering fields. We believe that learning happens when you get your hands dirty, as most successful enterprises started off in 'garages' from where we take inspiration. We are a group of multidisciplinary budding engineers trying to solve problems around us by cleverly applying our technical skills.

Script

Script is a collection of people with differing interests. Some are voracious readers, while others are avid cinephiles and ardent music fans. Our strength lies in our diversity, which we solidify by hosting regular discussions about various topics, both deep and trivial to gain an insight into the perspectives of others. This coalescence of multifarious personalities makes us who we are, and will continue to be the cornerstone of IE Script for decades to come.

Robotics

IE Robotics is an amalgamation of all SIGs, brought together by our common love for automation and bot building. Our members come from various technical backgrounds spanning IoT, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Mechanical Engineering and many more. Our vision is to create without any constraints and consequences, which is the cornerstone of IE Robotics

Tectonic

Tectonic is a newly born Special Interest Group of IE. We aim to create, improve, and design a brighter future by using our knowledge from Civil and Architecture. As the students with a shared interest in Construction and Designing but not restricted to those two, we discuss and share ideas regarding ongoing civil innovations and how to build a better tomorrow in a 'civil'ized manner.
